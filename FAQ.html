Frequently Asked Questions

Question: How often should I mow my lawn?
Answer: The frequency of lawn mowing depends on factors like grass type, season, and weather conditions. In general, mowing once a week during the growing season is recommended.
Question: What is landscape design, and why is it important?
Answer: Landscape design involves planning and arranging outdoor spaces for aesthetic and functional purposes. It is important as it enhances the overall appearance of a property, improves usability, and adds value.
Question: When is the best time to fertilize my lawn?
Answer: The timing of lawn fertilization depends on the type of fertilizer and your location. In general, early spring and fall are common times for fertilization to promote healthy growth.
