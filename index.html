About Me:

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, I developed a deep connection with nature in a small town.

My fondness for the outdoors and yard care has been a constant throughout my life Taking pride.

in my work brings me joy, especially when people passing by inquire about the authenticity of the

lush greenery, eliciting a smile from me. The California Trimmer has become my tool of choice,

impressing me with its precise cuts that give the lawn the appearance of a perfectly manicured turf. Maintaining

a routine, I cut the lawn every other day to keep it consistently low, contributing to its overall polished look.
